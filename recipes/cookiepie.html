Deep Dish Cookie Pie

Description

Who knew that you could have your pie and eat it guilt free?! This sweet delicious dessert is fudgy, chocolatey, and oh so satisfying.


Hidden in the center of this delicious indulgence is a secret ingredient that no one would ever guess. Protein rich beans, cheap, healthy, and entirely hidden

Ingredients

  • 2 cans white or garbanzo beans
  • 1 cup quick oats or almond flour
  • 1/4 cup applesauce or yogurt
  • 3 tbsp oil or 1/4 cup nut butter
  • 2 tsp pure vanilla extract
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 1/2 cup brown or alternative sugar
  • 1 cup chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Drain and rinse beans very well.
  2. Blend everything but the chocolate chips in food processor or blender
  3. Combine with chocolate chips
  4. Pour into greased pan.
  5. Cook at 350'F for 35 minutes
  6. Let stand at least 10 minutes before removing from pan. Serve hot, or allow to cool
